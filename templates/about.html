Our Story

Welcome to Garage Sale Organizer! I am a passionate computer science student, working on my capstone project under the guidance of Dr. Kinsman. This undertaking is a result of my desire to combine technical skills and the love for community engagement while completing the requirements of my master's degree. With Dr. Kinsman's guidance and insights, I embarked on this capstone project with a mission: to create a web-based tool that aims to deliver a reliable, user-friendly and scalable solution for Garage Sale enthusiasts. This project is not just about building a tool. It's about enhancing the local community experience. I believe that garage sales are not just about buying and selling; they are opportunities for making meaningful connections. I am committed to delivering a user-friendly, efficient, and enjoyable platform that helps users find the best garage sales near them and provides organizers with the tools they need to connect with their community.
